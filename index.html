About Me

Phil Joseph Mendoza

I am a graduate of BS-Information Systems at De La Salle-College of Saint Benilde batch 2016. Currently working at the family business (Local market meat shop) and studying programming at Zuitt Coding Bootcamp.

My Story

I have 2 siblings, in which I am the youngest. When choosing the degree I want to pursue in college, my parents encouraged me to take on Civil Engineering since it is their dream to build our own properties from scratch and for us to manage them by ourselves. My eldest sister graduated with a degree of BS - Interior Design while my other sister has BS - Hotel, Restaurant and Institution Management where I will construct the building, my eldest sister will design the interiors of it, and the middle sibling will manage it. When I was in my first year in college, it was hard for me because I never really had the knack to construct and build any physical structures or things so I didn't see a future of me as a Civil Engineer. That's when I decided to shift course and study BS - Information Systems.

After graduating, I wasn't able to enter the IT industry since I was needed at our family business ever since the 2019 pandemic happened. After a year and 8 months, I finally got time to start my career as a web developer since my brother-in-law decided to help out in the family business.

Since I was not able to practice and exercise the programming skills that I learned back in college, I looked up coding bootcamps in the Philippines. Now I am currently a student of Zuitt Coding Bootcamp where I can re-learn and further hone my skills in programming.

Until now, I am grateful for the path that I chose and I want to continue my life as a future Software/Web Developer.
